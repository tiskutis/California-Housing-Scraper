Description for 18451 Homestead Ave

Come live in The Preserves at Vista Del Verde built by Toll Brothers located at 18451 Homestead Ave Yorba Linda, Orange County CA. Vista Del Verde is an 879-acre community nestled in the hills of Yorba Linda among the lush fairways of the beautiful 18-hole championship Black Gold Golf Club. This beautiful home offers 4 bedrooms, 2.5 baths with 2,500 square feet of luxury living space built in 2012, it can be yours. The oversized master suite offers a lavish bath featuring a dual-sink vanity and gracious walk-in closet. Beautiful family room, living room with fireplace, plantation shutters, custom paint, epoxy flooring in the garage, water softener, and much more. The well-appointed kitchen has an upgraded full backsplash granite counter-top, stained maple cabinets, island with breakfast bar, and KitchenAid stainless steel appliances. Garden like low maintenance backyard, with beautiful fountains, fire-pit, and multiple fruit trees. Solar energy, monthly lease of $42 per month. Experience the beauty and serenity of its natural setting with the convenience of Orange County’s business, recreation, and entertainment opportunities only minutes away.
